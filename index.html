Head Pose and Eye Gaze Prediction for Driver Attention Research


Driver's gaze direction is an indicator of driver state and plays a significantly role in driving safety. Traditional gaze zone estimation methods based on eye model have disadvantages due to the vulnerability under large head movement. Different from these methods, an appearance-based head pose and eye gaze prediction method is proposed in this research, for driver gaze zone estimation under free head movement...
